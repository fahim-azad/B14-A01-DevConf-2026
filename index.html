Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register?

You can register by clicking the "Register Now" button in the navigation bar or the hero section. We offer Standard, Pro, and Team packages.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, we offer full refunds up to 30 days before the event. After that, tickets are non-refundable but can be transferred to a colleague.

Will sessions be recorded?

Yes, all keynote and track sessions will be recorded. Standard ticket holders get 30-day access, while Pro and Team ticket holders get lifetime access.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes, all attendees will receive a digital certificate of attendance after the conference concludes.

Where is the venue?

DevConf 2026 will be held at the Moscone Center in San Francisco, CA. The venue is fully accessible and near major transit hubs.
